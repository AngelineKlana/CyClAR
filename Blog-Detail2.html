15 Tour de France Winners

THE YOUNG COLOMBIAN'S TALENT, EGAN BERNAL, SEALS TOUR DE SUISSE VICTORY. AWESOME ACHIEVEMENT AT PARIS-NICE 2020.

Conquering the triumph in cycling's biggest bike race at 22 makes Egan Bernal the youngest winner in post-war TDF history. The Colombian's victory is the most prestigious jewel of those obtained so far by the brand new DOGMA F12. Success that brings to 15th the number of the yellow jersey dressed in Paris by riders riding on CyclAR. End of discussion... again. Egan is also the fourth rider to win the Tour for the Team Sky/Ineos formation since 2012, after B.Wiggins, C.Froome and G.Thomas. The British team stretches their record of domination of the France race to seven triumphs in eight years.

Egan Bernal put in an assured ride on the final day of the Tour de Suisse to confirm overall victory leading to the first World Tour success the Brand New Dogma F12. The colombian was able to follow a late attack from the rivals on the Furkapass and reamained glued to the australian's Rohan Dennis wheel to cross the line 3rd, securing a 19 second GC win. Once again Team Ineos put up a staunch defence of the yellow jersey, with Luke Rowe and Ben Swift riding hard to keep the race together as attacks began on the first climb of the day. Congrats Team Ineos.

Egan Bernal secured overall victory at Paris-Nice after a tense final stage which saw his yellow jersey come under attack by fellow countryman Nairo Quintana. Bernal enjoyed the perfect work of several Team Sky teammates to defend the primacy. For the 22-year-old it's his second WorldTour stage race win after the Tour of California last year. It's the first win of the season, who is set to be a team leader at the Giro d'Italia. Another huge achievement for Cicli Pinarello #F10NaturalBornWinner.
