Product Description and Specification

Carbon T1100 UD Dream Carbon with Nanoalloy Technology with NANOALLOY Technology, Asymmetric frame, Italian thread BB, Drop In bearing system with tapered headset ( 1” 1/8 up; 1” 1/2 down), “Hold On Shoulder” top tube, Flexstays, AERO seat post, Think 2 to fit electronical or mechanical groupsets on the same frame, ICR internal cable routing (BB guide removed), FSC Frontal seat clamp, integrated and aerodynamic, Flatback profiles, E-link, Possibility to mount both double and single chainring (removing the front derailleur hanger), UCI approved, RAD SYSTEM disc brakes and Disc Flat Mount (max Ø160 mm)
