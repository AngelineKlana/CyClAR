About Us

The history of CyclAR in producing the highest-quality bike in the world.

1989

The Beginning of CyclAR

Gio Rello was born in Catena di Villorba on the 10th July, 1922 during a difficult post-war period in Italy. He was the eighth of twelve children. His love for two wheels and an innate gift as an indomitable fighter, led him to pursue a career in cycling. This is the ‘Eroico’ (Heroic) era in cycling: Coppi, Bartali, Bobet on white roads, difficult, very difficult terrain. But the young Nani immediately attracted attention, winning over 60 races and the tender age of 20 won “la Popolarissima" in Treviso, an important race in the Dilettanti circuit ( a level of racing which opens doors to being a Professional)

1991-1993

The First Tour de France

The first Tour de France arrive with Pedro Delgado, Cicli Pinarello is changing, it's no longer the small workshop, now it starts to get serious. The "Rosse" enters through the main door in the cycling that counts. The 90s are beginning, this decade will mark the fate of the Treviso brand. Giovanni slowly leaves his place to Fausto, who will continue the dream.

1995-2001

Greatest Competition in History

The reign of Miguel Indurain begins: 4 Tour de France, Giro d'Italia, Olympic Gold Medal, World Championship. the Grande Navarro will forever be the ambassador of Pinarello in the World. Team Telekom, the German Armada arrives: two more Tour de France, World TT Championships and Olympic Gold Medals. The Pinarello bikes dominate everywhere.
